ABOUT ELON MUSK

Entrepreneur • Engineer • Innovator

Elon Musk is the founder and CEO of companies such as SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. Known for his ambitious vision, he pushes the boundaries of technology in electric vehicles, space exploration, and renewable energy. His work is driven by the mission to make life multiplanetary and to accelerate the transition to sustainable energy.
